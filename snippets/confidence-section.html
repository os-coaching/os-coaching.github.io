And if it’s not about the job

Maybe it’s the beach photos you keep dodging.

Keeping your shirt on at the pool. Standing at the back of every photo. Turning down the holiday because of how you feel about your body.

That’s not a discipline problem either — and it’s nothing to be ashamed of. The same three levers, run consistently, are how you walk into summer without a plan for hiding. Leo and Finn started exactly there.
